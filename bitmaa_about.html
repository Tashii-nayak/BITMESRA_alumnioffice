Birla Institute of Technology Alumni Association ( BITMAA )

The BIT Mesra Alumni Association (BITMAA) is a vibrant network that unites former students of Birla Institute of Technology (BIT), Mesra, fostering a strong, lifelong connection to the institution and its global community. BITMAA aims to create an ecosystem where alumni can support each other through professional networking, mentorship, and collaboration across diverse fields. The association organizes events, reunions, and networking sessions, bringing alumni together to share knowledge, celebrate achievements, and inspire the next generation of graduates. 

In addition to personal and professional support, BITMAA collaborates with BIT Mesra to contribute to the institute’s growth and development, including initiatives to improve educational resources, career opportunities, and innovation. The association also manages an online portal to facilitate easy communication and access to resources for alumni. BITMAA is committed to nurturing the legacy of BIT Mesra, building on its heritage while fostering new opportunities for its community members.
