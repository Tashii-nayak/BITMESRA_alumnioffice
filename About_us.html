Alumni Office

The Alumni and International Relations Office at BIT Mesra serves as a vital link between the institution, its alumni, and the international community. Committed to lifelong engagement, it organizes alumni networking events, professional mentorship, and career support programs that enhance students’ industry exposure and opportunities. The office facilitates international collaborations, promoting academic exchanges and partnerships with universities worldwide. By connecting alumni with current students, the office builds a robust support system that fosters professional growth, mentorship, and knowledge-sharing. Through these initiatives, the office empowers students and alumni to thrive in their careers, contribute to BIT’s legacy, and strengthen global ties.
