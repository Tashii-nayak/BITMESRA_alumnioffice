Birla Institute of Technology Mesra Alumni Association (BITMAA)

The Birla Institute of Technology Mesra Alumni Association (BITMAA) serves as a strong and dynamic link between the alumni and the institution. BITMAA aims to bring together the alumni of BIT Mesra to foster a network of professionals that contribute to the academic, professional, and social development of the institute. The association promotes alumni engagement through events, networking opportunities, and career support initiatives, ensuring that alumni remain connected with their alma mater and continue contributing to its growth. BITMAA also supports the current students of BIT Mesra through mentorship programs and career counseling.
