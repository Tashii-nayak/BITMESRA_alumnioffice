SARC - Student Alumni Relations Cell

SARC is a collective of  BIT MESRA students who are active in facilitating the connection between the alma mater and the alumni body. These youngsters work as a team to reach out to the alumni members. They strive to update the alumni database on a regular basis, so that no BIT-ian is left out of the larger family. The SARC members diligently assist in the organization of alumni events. The dedicated graphics team of SARC creates and publicizes content related to alumni engagement, achievement, and contribution
